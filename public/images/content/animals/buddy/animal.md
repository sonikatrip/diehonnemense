## Story
Buddy was hit by a car and left on the roadside. A passerby found him and called for help. His injuries were severe, but his will to live was stronger. The first thing he did when he woke up from surgery was lick the vet's hand.

## Current Situation
Buddy has defied the odds and recovered completely. He walks with a slight wobble but it doesn't dampen his enthusiasm for life. Buddy is proof that love and care can heal even the worst injuries.
